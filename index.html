How to Play

  • On each turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as they wish.
  • Each roll adds to their current score.
  • Roll a 1 and you lose your current score — turn passes to the other player.
  • Choose Hold to bank your current score into your total.
  • First player to reach 100 points wins!
